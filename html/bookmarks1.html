A central processing unit (CPU) is the hardware within a computer that carries out instructions of a computer program by performing the basic arithmetical logical and input/output operations of the system.the term has been in use in the computer industry at least since the early 1960. the form the design the implementations of cpus hav changed ovr the course of their history but their fundamental operations remains much the same.

ALU

An arithmetic logic unit (ALU) is a digital circuit used to perform arithmetic and logic operations. It represents the fundamental building block of the central processing unit (CPU) of a computer and even the simplest microprocessors contain one for purposes such as maintaining timers. the processors found inside modern cpu and graphic processing units(gpu) accomodate very powerfull and very complex alu a single component may contain a number of alu.
